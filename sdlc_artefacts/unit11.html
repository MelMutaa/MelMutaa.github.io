Introduction to Secure Software Development

Learning Overview

Learning Outcomes:
  • Examine future trends in secure design, development, programming languages, and operating systems.
  • Understand the concepts of Fog Computing, the Internet of Things (IoT), and Cyber Physical Systems.
  • Explore potential systems and solutions that could serve as research topics or areas of investigation.

On completion of this unit, you will be able to:
  • Provide examples of components and solutions related to Fog Computing, IoT, and Cyber Physical Systems.
  • Identify key security challenges associated with these systems.
  • Recommend emerging technologies and solutions worthy of further investigation.
