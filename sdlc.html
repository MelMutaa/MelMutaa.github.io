Secure Software Development

Explore the learning outcomes, skills gained, unit summaries, artefacts, team notes, feedback, reflections and action plans of this module.

Learning Outcomes

  • Gain a critical understanding of abstraction in programming.
  • Learn secure development methodologies.
  • Explore software development processes, including analysis, design, construction, and testing, including the use of UML diagrams for visualization.
  • Understand architecture and SDLC models, like TOGAF and Agile.
  • Practice teamwork, conflict resolution, and critical evaluation of approaches.
  • Reflect on and assess personal development.

Skills Gained

Risk Management

I am able to identify and manage security risks in software development.

Criticality

I have devloped the ability to critically analyse problems and choose suitable methodologies and tools.

Leadership

I have developed teamworking skills in a virtual professional environment with real-world perspectives.

Technical

I can design, develop, and evaluate software solutions.
